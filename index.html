What is EquiTrade?

EquiTrade is a platform that aims to use machine learning to fuel algorithmic trading.

While existing solutions offered my hedge funds allow people to invest in algorithmic-fuelled trading, & we aim to build an easy to use platform for anyone to use. It will include easy payment integration, and insights and analytics on stocks to watch.

At the end, our product aims to level the playing field to allow for anyone regardless of skill to invest, using the help of our model which predicts strong investments for the user.

The App apps

Once you’ve entered into our ecosystem, you can mange everything. Anyone with a smartphone and an internet connection can use our analytics platform to make investments.

Our app aims to use deep learning regression models to predict future stock prices. Currently, the model works using time-series data from the S&P 500, but will be expanding on to other markets soon.

What is EquiTrade?

EquiTrade - is an unique platform; that aims to use deep learning models to predict stock prices and make trading decisions. Our analytics platform aims to provide clear, consistent insight on the user's portfolio and aims to advise them on investment decisions.

What financial markets and investments do EquiTrade work for?

EquiTrade currently works on the S&P 500, but aims to expand to other financial markets such as the NASDAQ, and other investments such as cryptocurrencies in the near future..

How can I use EquiTrade?

EquiTrade aims to launch an online platform and be released on mobile within the near future. To sign up for updates, use the contact form below!.

How do I benefit from EquiTrade's platform?

EquiTrade aims to help consumers invest in various financial markets by providing insight and analytics on their portfolio, and calculate estimated returns and recommend buy and sell decisions. The platform is aimed to be used by a wide range of consumers, from financial managers to normal consumers.
